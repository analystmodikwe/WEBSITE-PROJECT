1 About the business and web site 

Thokoza's car wash is a business founded by a black South African, in the townships of thokoza and it has many franchises in that whole township. Unlike most car washes in the townships. They get many customers but can't maintain the number of cars they get because they don't have a system, that's where i come in with the website to solve that problem. 

I created the website to help the business take their business digitally, so that a lot of their customers can schedule a car wash session from the conform of their home or wherever they are, and even some of the people who want to try out the car-wash can just look at it from the website first, with going there physically just to know things like prices. And the good thing about having a website is everyone can see it no matter where they are, and it's good for business especially when it comes to things like running promos and marketing. 

The target audience is 

Both youth and elders who are vehicle owners and i want them to buy and inquire 

The devices used will be a mix of mobile phones (mostly) and desktop computers, or laptops 

Needs: Quick access to service prices, contact information, and location 

Goals: Book a car wash, find pricing, or get directions to the business

Competitor Analysis 

Website 1  Shield Express  

Pages They Have 

This is a big company with a lot of pages. They have a Home page, a About page, a Service Menu page, a Products page, a Locations page, a Gallery page, a Franchise Opportunity page, and even an MSDS page. They also have a Specials page under the Service Menu.  

How They Present Information 

The first thing you see when you land on their homepage is a big image slider that rotates through announcements about new branches opening and franchise opportunities. Below, they have short sections with icons and a heading like "FRANCHISE OPPORTUNITY" or "QUALITY SERVICE" with a small paragraph and a button. At the very top before the navbar they show their phone number and email address so customers can contact them immediately without having to search for it. 

Design and Layout 

They use a yellow, gold-looking color as their brand color which stands out against the dark and white backgrounds they use. The navbar has dropdown menus for some pages like Services and Automatic Car Wash Machines. The overall feel is professional and established, like a brand you can trust. 

What I Thought Worked Well 

Putting the phone number at the very top of every page is smart because customers do not have to click to a contact page just to call 

The Locations page has a Google Maps embed for every single branch which makes it very easy to find them 

They have a Gallery page with YouTube videos of actual car washes which builds trust with new customers 

What I Think Could Be Better 

The navbar has too many links and it feels overwhelming when you first visit 

Their pricing is hidden you must click through several pages before finding out how much a wash cost

Website 3 Auto Image 

Pages They Have 

They have a Home page, a Services page with three sub-pages (Car Wash, Auto Protection, and Innov8 Performance), an About page, a Contact page, a Go Green page, and a Franchise page. The Go Green page is interesting because it shows they care about the environment which is something customers today appreciate. 

How They Present Information 

The homepage opens with a large full screen hero image and then flows into a section about who they are, followed by a grid of four service cards. After that they have a customer testimonials section with real quotes from real customers. The footer has all the contact details, address, and trading hours in one place, so customers do not have to go searching. 

Design and Layout 

They use dark backgrounds with white text and large images. The navbar is clean with a dropdown under Services. What I noticed most is that they have a WhatsApp button that floats on the page and appears multiple times encouraging customers to book via WhatsApp.  

 

 

What I Thought Worked Well 

The customer testimonials with real names make the business feel trustworthy 

The floating WhatsApp booking button makes it incredibly easy for customers to get in touch 

Trading hours are displayed clearly in the footer, so customers always know when they are open 

What I Think Could Be Better 

There are no prices anywhere on the website which is frustrating as a potential customer 

There is no online booking form — everything goes through WhatsApp which might not suit everyone
